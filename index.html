Here is my CV.
I am a PhD candidate at NYU's philosophy department, advised by Cian Dorr. My main research interests are in the philosophy of science, metaphysics, and the philosophy of physics. I'm especially interested in causation, chance, and laws. My dissertation explores what causal loops can teach us about the nature of chance and causation—a lot, I argue.

Before coming to NYU, I did my undergraduate degree in physics at ETH Zurich, followed by a B.Phil. in philosophy at Oxford.
