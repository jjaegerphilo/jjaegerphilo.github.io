Here is my CV.
I am an assistant professor in the department of philosophy at the University of Texas at Austin. I work in the philosophy of science, metaphysics, and the philosophy of physics, with particular interests in causation, chance, laws, and statistical mechanics.

Before coming to UT Austin, I did my PhD at NYU, a BPhil in philosophy at Oxford, and a BSc in physics at ETH Zurich.
