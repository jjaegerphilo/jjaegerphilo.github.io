Here is my CV.
I am a PhD candidate at NYU's philosophy department. My main research interests are in the philosophy of science, metaphysics, and the philosophy of physics. I'm especially interested in causation, chance, laws, and spacetime.

My dissertation examines causal loops and what they can teach us about the nature of chance and causation—a lot, I argue. I'm advised by advised by Cian Dorr, Tim Maudlin, and Michael Strevens.

Before coming to NYU, I did my undergraduate degree in physics at ETH Zurich, followed by a B.Phil. in philosophy at Oxford.
